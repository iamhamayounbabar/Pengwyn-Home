Pengwyn BINC NFT Collections

Pengwyn BINC focuses on building a cross-chain management protocol where different NFTs have their own dedicated status and share power in the company architecture. These NFTs fall into three main NFT collections which are designed to create different roles and utilities for their holders.

Pengwyn OG NFT Collection

Pengwyn OG NFT Collection is the initial collection of Pengwyn BINC and consists of 111 hand-drawn Pengwyn NFTs.

The collection is categorized into three primary character pools: Members, Councilors, and the Ultimate Pengwyn. These divisions represent distinct tiers within the Pengwyn Council, functioning as both a prominent cult in the storyline and the board of directors in the company.

Pengwyn OG NFTs hold the most substantial share power per NFT in Pengwyn BINC and carry significant roles and utilities within the ecosystem.

Pengwyn Waddle NFT Collection

Pengwyn Waddle NFT Collection consists of 10.000 generative Pengwyn NFTs. The collection is subdivided into four equally supplied editions, with each edition featuring distinct Pengwyn classes originating from four different universes.

To become a cross-chain development hub, each edition of the Pengwyn Waddle NFT Collection is strategically designed to be minted on four distinct blockchains. Each edition sales serves as progressive capital increments for the company.
