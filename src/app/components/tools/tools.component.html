Pengwyn Autonomous Organization

Pengwyn Autonomous Organization (PAO) serves as a voting tool that automates withdrawals from the PAO Vault with the approval of Pengwyn BINC shareholders. PAO tool facilitates the decision-making process regarding expenditures within the organization.

To use the collateralized funds in PAO Vault, the team is required to create detailed submissions that clarifies the purpose of the expenditure and the necessary capital amount.

Pengwyn Stats

Pengwyn Stats is a comprehensive value dashboard that is designed to demonstrate various valuations of Pengwyn BINC NFTs and offers additional financial data for the shareholders of the company.

By prioritizing transparency and implementing different measures, Pengwyn BINC enables a secure investment and trading experience within the NFT ecosystem.

Pengwyn Sales Force Program

The Pengwyn Sales Force Program (PSFP) is an active income tool that empowers Pengwyn BINC NFT owners to act as intermediaries in the Pengwyn Waddle NFT Collection and partner projects' mint sales.

To engage in mints and sales, NFT owners can generate promotion codes that grant mint rights and discounts for investors of specific projects.

Suggestion Votings

Pengwyn Suggestion is an on-chain voting tool that empowers Pengwyn BINC shareholders to propose suggestions and enables the community to vote on these ideas.

This tool is a crucial component of Pengwyn BINC's decentralization efforts as it provides valuable insights into the community's expectations.
