What is Pengwyn BINC?

Pengwyn BINC is a blockchain-based company that leverages NFTs and other on-chain assets to establish a mutually manageable platform across four distinct blockchains.

The primary focus of Pengwyn BINC is to develop a transparent management environment and position itself as an innovative blockchain company that engages in both development and investment activities. In order to grant ownership rights and assign various roles within the management structure, the project provides a range of assets to its investors.

Pengwyn BINC Assets

A sustainable business economy

Pengwyn BINC is built upon a sustainable economy model that aims to provide shareholders with active and passive income over the short, medium, and long terms. All categories of protocol revenue are distributed among the shareholders and the company vaults.

The distribution takes place either as daily rewards or upon profit realization.

Pengwyn BINC VAULTS

The business model is supported by three main vaults which work as different budgets of the company. All assets that are secured in the company vaults are 100% transparent to Pengwyn BINC asset owners on Pengwyn DAPP.

Treasury Vault

Pengwyn Treasury Vault functions as a fund to cover the operational expenses of Pengwyn BINC in the short term. 7.5% of the mint revenue will be directly deposited into the Vault and will be controlled by the team.

PAO Vault

PAO Vault serves as a collateralized fund which focuses on medium and long term development expenses. 50% of the mint revenue will be deposited into this vault and the team can only use it with the permission of the shareholders.

Investment Vault

Investment Vault functions as a budget which is reserved for long term investments of Pengwyn BINC. 20% of the mint revenue will be directly deposited into the Investment Vault and will be controlled by the team.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) are digital assets that are stored on Blockchain and are linked to a digital content. Pengwyn BINC transforms NFTs into ownership assets in a company structure.

BINC in the context of Pengwyn means Blockchain Incorporation.

Pengwyn BINC treats each blockchain as a separate universe. The idea is to build a story where Pengwyns and projects from different blockchains actually take parts in the story script. The story will be written by a professional script writer and is planned to be released in the form of digital comic book series within the blockchain.

Pengwyn BINC is designed to be managed on four distinct blockchains. To be able to achieve this goal, the project requires the interoperability technology by ICON Blockchain. In other words, Pengwyn BINC will be powered by xCall and BTP.

After finding a proper launch base, the team will offer these blockchains. The final decision will be made by the Pengwyns using the Pengwyn Suggestion Voting tool on the Pengwyn DAPP.
